History

Exciting Expeditions was born from a love for the outdoors and a passion for teamwork. Founded by a group of river guides in the early 90s, we started with just one raft and a dream to share the untamed beauty of the canyons with the world. Our journey began on the banks of the Snake River, where every rapid told a story of courage.

From our humble beginnings to our current status as a leader in travel, we have never lost our focus on safety and thrills. Today, we operate across the most challenging rivers, providing premium rafting experiences. Our mission remains the same: to create unforgettable memories through the power of white water.
